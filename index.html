Official Manifesto

The fundamental purpose of this platform is to provide absolute transparency regarding my personal trading activity. By sharing both live trade details as they occur and a comprehensive historical audit of my performance, I aim to achieve the following objectives:

  • Professional Accountability: This repository serves as my primary professional ledger—an unfiltered, objective record of my career.
  • Radical Transparency: I provide raw performance data, ensuring aspiring traders can conduct a realistic evaluation of the profession, dispelling myths about "easy" wealth.
  • Sustainable Infrastructure: Partner broker links provide essential support, creating a crucial buffer for market drawdowns.
  • Community Reinvestment: In periods of sustained success, I leverage surplus capital to facilitate community-driven initiatives, providing startup capital to help others replicate this methodology.
