Official Manifesto

The fundamental purpose of this platform is to provide absolute transparency regarding my personal trading activity. By sharing both live trade details as they occur and a comprehensive historical audit of my performance, I aim to achieve the following objectives:

  • A) Professional Accountability: As a full-time trader, this repository serves as my primary professional ledger. It provides an unfiltered, objective record of my career, functioning as a definitive performance profile.
  • B) Radical Transparency: I provide raw, unedited performance data. My goal is to showcase the reality of the markets—losses included—to ensure aspiring traders can conduct a realistic evaluation of the profession, dispelling the myth that day trading is an effortless path to quick wealth.
  • C) Sustainable Infrastructure: I provide access to partner broker (Introducing Broker) links. If a visitor utilizes these links to establish a professional account and engages in live trading, a portion of the generated trading commissions is allocated to support my trading efforts. This infrastructure provides a crucial buffer, particularly during challenging market drawdowns or losing streaks.
  • D) Community Reinvestment: In periods of sustained performance excellence, I intend to leverage surplus capital to facilitate community-driven initiatives. This includes providing entry-level trading capital (starting at $400) to other traders, enabling them to replicate my methodology and gain the necessary experience to build their own professional journey.
