Blueprint

Strategic framework for asset growth and risk mitigation within the system.

Daily Soft Goal

  • Execute 5 winning trades minimum per trading day
  • Each trade target: 6% ROI per trade
  • Compound wins throughout the day for exponential growth
  • Stop trading once daily goal is achieved

Per Trade Entry Strategy

  • Confluence-based entry points
  • Support/Resistance validation
  • Risk-to-reward minimum 1:2 ratio

Position Management

  • Fixed 2% risk per trade (from account balance)
  • Per trade profit target: 6% ROI TP
  • Per trade stop loss: 6% ROI SL
  • Scale-out strategy on winners for risk management

Execution Protocol

  • MT5 institutional execution only
  • Market hours: High liquidity zones only
  • Technical analysis driven entries
  • Zero emotional decision-making
  • Compounding wins = reinvest gains into next trade
